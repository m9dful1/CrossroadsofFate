## Introduction

SpiritWise Studios ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile game application "Crossroads of Fate" ("the App," "our App," or "the Game") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our services.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

**Local Game Data:** The App automatically collects and stores the following information locally on your device:
- Game progress (current scenario, completed scenarios)
- Player inventory (items collected during gameplay)
- Quest progress (active and completed quests)
- Locations visited within the game
- Player choices and decision history
- Game settings and preferences

**Technical Data:** The App may generate the following technical information:
- Error logs and crash reports (stored locally for debugging purposes)
- Application performance data (stored locally)
- Debug information during development builds

### Information We Do NOT Collect

**Personal Information:** We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as:
- Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
- Location data from your device
- Contact information
- Payment information
- Social media accounts

**Device Information:** We do not access or collect:
- Photos, videos, or other media files
- Contacts or address book
- SMS messages or call logs
- Device identifiers beyond what's necessary for local storage

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Storage Only
All game data is stored locally on your device using Android's Room database system. This data is used exclusively to:
- Save and restore your game progress
- Maintain game state between sessions
- Provide personalized gameplay experience
- Track quest completion and inventory management

### Error Logging
Local error logs are created solely for:
- Improving game stability and performance
- Identifying and fixing bugs
- Enhancing user experience

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

**No Data Sharing:** We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer any of your information to third parties. All data remains on your device.

**No Third-Party Services:** The App does not integrate with:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Social media platforms
- Cloud storage services
- Online gaming services

## Data Security

### Local Storage Security
- All game data is stored using Android's secure local database (Room)
- Data is protected by your device's security measures
- No data transmission occurs over networks
- Application follows Android security best practices

### Device Protection
We recommend you:
- Keep your device updated with the latest security patches
- Use device lock screens and security features
- Be cautious when installing apps from unknown sources

## Data Retention

### Game Progress Data
- Game data persists until you uninstall the application
- You can reset your progress using the in-app reset function
- Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local game data

### Log Files
- Error logs are automatically managed and limited in size (1MB maximum)
- Logs are automatically truncated when they exceed size limits
- Logs are deleted when the app is uninstalled

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Control
You have complete control over your data:
- **Access:** View your game progress in the character menu and map screens
- **Modification:** Change your progress by playing the game
- **Deletion:** Reset progress using in-app controls or uninstall the app
- **Portability:** Data remains on your device; no cloud backup is performed

### Game Settings
You can:
- Reset your game progress at any time
- Start new games without affecting previous progress
- Delete the app to remove all data permanently

## Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for children's data. However, we recommend parental guidance for younger users.
